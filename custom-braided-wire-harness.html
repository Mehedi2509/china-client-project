Material recommendation

QL-Custom Technology Ltd. have many choices for wire harness braiding material. Each one is good for different jobs. We will help you choose the best one for your Custom braided wire harness project.

PET

PET braid is made from plastic. It's light, strong, and low-cost. It bends easily and fits around many shapes. It stands up to heat, dirt, oil, and sun. You can use it inside or outside. It's an excellent choice for general jobs.

Nylon

The nylon braid is firm and rigid. It resists high heat, oil, water, and acid. It keeps its shape and color even after prolonged use. It's excellent for engines, tools, and heavy jobs. It works well in places with lots of action and wear.

Tinned copper shielding sleeve

This braid is made from tiny copper wires coated with tin. It blocks electric noise and keeps power signals clean. It's best for wires that carry signals, power, or data. It also adds strength and protects from rubbing.

Military green nylon braided mesh tube

This is made with strong nylon in army green. It's built for defense systems. It resists heat, dirt, and water. It fits tight places and works well in planes, radios, tanks, etc.

Solutions

We build the harness that fits your needs. First, we place and group the wires. Then, we twist them if needed. After that, we add the braid. This keeps wires in place, stops rubbing, and adds long life to the harness.

Twisted Wire Harness Twisted

Some jobs need extra strength. In that case, we twist smaller wire pairs, then twist them again into bigger sets. After that, we add the braid. This makes the harness very strong, neat, and flexible. It's suitable for tough places.

Twisting wires

Twisting wires helps keep them tight and neat. It stops them from moving too much or getting messy. When wires are twisted, the braid stays snug. This adds a clean and pro look to your harness.

Customizable braiding density

You can choose how tight or loose you want the braid to be. If you need strong protection, go with a tight braid. If you need more bend, a loose braid is better. We use a skilled wire harness braider to meet your exact needs.
